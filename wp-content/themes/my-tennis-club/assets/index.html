WHAT IS INCLUDED IN OUR FEE?

Instruction: 21 hours of tennis instruction, 4 hours of fitness, 4 hours of yoga, evening activities, 12 meals, snacks, housing, seminars, guest speakers, take-home information pamphlet, prizes, a t-shirt and more!

02.-

HOW DO I KNOW IF I REGISTERED?

Following registration, a confirmation email will be sent to ensure your place. If you have not received your confirmation email, check your spam or contact us at bajoitennis@gmail.com.

03.-

WHAT IF I NEED TO CANCEL?

Refunds are available up to the deadline of one week prior to the camp start date. In the event of injury, provide documentation for a refund after the non-refundable deposit date.
